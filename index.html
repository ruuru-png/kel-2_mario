Here's an introduction to Super Mario in English: Super Mario: The Iconic Hero of the Mushroom Kingdom Super Mario is one of the most famous, best-selling, and beloved video game franchises of all time, created by legendary Japanese game designer Shigeru Miyamoto for Nintendo. At its core, the games revolve around the adventures of a small Italian plumber named Mario (often accompanied by his younger brother, Luigi), who is tasked with rescuing Princess Peach from his archnemesis, King Bowser the Koopa, across various fantastical worlds, primarily the Mushroom Kingdom.

Mario is an iconic character created by Nintendo, first introduced in the arcade game "Donkey Kong" in 1981. Originally designed as a simple pixelated character known as "Jumpman," Mario was tasked with rescuing a damsel in distress named Pauline from the clutches of the giant ape, Donkey Kong. This marked the beginning of Mario's journey, establishing him as a heroic figure in the gaming world. Over the years, Mario has evolved into a symbol of the gaming industry, representing not only Nintendo but also the broader culture of video gaming. His cheerful demeanor, distinctive red hat, and blue overalls have made him instantly recognizable to audiences around the globe.
